# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the Hospital AI Assistant community will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Offensive comments related to gender, identity, religion, race, etc.
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Community leaders will review and investigate all complaints. They are obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.

### Consequences

- **Warning**: For minor violations
- **Temporary Ban**: For serious violations (30 days)
- **Permanent Ban**: For repeated or severe violations

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. Email: report@hospital-ai-assistant.dev
2. Include details and any relevant context
3. Do not share on public channels initially

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled professionally.

---

**By participating in this project, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.**
