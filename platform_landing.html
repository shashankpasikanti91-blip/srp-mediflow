Telegram Notifications

How Notifications Work

Each hospital owns its own Telegram bot. Staff and patients are notified automatically — no manual work.

👳 Staff Group Alerts

1

Create a Telegram Bot via @BotFather

Open Telegram → search @BotFather → send /newbot → follow steps → copy the token

7123456789:AABBccDDeeFF...
2

Add Bot to Your Staff Telegram Group

Create a Telegram group for your hospital staff → add your bot as a member → make it an admin (so it can send messages)

3

Get the Staff Group Chat ID

Open this URL in browser (replace TOKEN):

https://api.telegram.org/botTOKEN/getUpdates

Look for "chat":{"id":-1001234567890} — the negative number is your Chat ID.

4

Paste Both in Admin → Notifications

Paste Bot Token + Staff Chat ID → click Save All Settings. Both fields are required for staff alerts to work.

👤 Patient Notifications

1

Patient Finds Your Hospital Bot

Display your bot username (e.g. @starhospital_bot) on your reception desk, website, or notice board.

2

Patient Sends /start to Your Bot

When the patient sends any message to your bot, the system records their Telegram Chat ID automatically — no manual entry needed.

3

System Auto-Notifies Them Forever

Every appointment confirmation, bill receipt, lab result, and reminder is sent directly to the patient's Telegram from that point on.

📝 Summary:
Bot Token + Staff Chat ID → staff group gets emergency alerts & updates
Patient sends /start to bot → patient gets personal notifications

Chat ID is required for staff group messages. Without it, only the bot token is saved but no one receives messages.
